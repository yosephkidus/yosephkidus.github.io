My Work

Throughout my modeling journey, I've had the privilege to walk the runway and collaborate with prestigious events and institutions. My participation in "One Africa Fest", "Afro Figure Fashion Week", and the celebrated "Lemat Fashion Show" has helped me to refine and showcase my unique style. Being part of "Mister Culture Third General" was another milestone that enriched my understanding of the intricate fusion of culture and fashion. Additionally, my training at the "International Certified Afro Figure Modelling and Art School" has provided me with the foundational skills and knowledge that I bring to each performance. Every stride I take on the runway, from these platforms to my work with Ambassador, emanates passion, leaving an indelible mark on the audience.
